Our Story

The Journey So Far

Milestones that shaped our culinary mission

2020

The Beginning

Founded with a vision to bring authentic wellness food to modern lifestyles, starting with traditional recipes from our founders' family heritage.

2021

First Kitchen

Launched our signature Moringa Soup and Traditional Dal, receiving overwhelming response from wellness enthusiasts.

2022

Expanding Menu

Added Thai Green Curry, ice creams, and seasonal wellness dishes, partnering with 100+ organic farmers across India.

2023

Sustainability Focus

Achieved 100% plastic-free packaging and launched our sustainability initiative, supporting regenerative farming practices.

2024

Community Growth

Reached 50,000+ happy customers and launched our meal subscription service to share wellness through food.
